About Me

Hello! I’m Jianne Soto, a recent UC Santa Cruz graduate with a BA in Art & Design: Games and Playable Media (minor in Technology & Information Management). I’m passionate about crafting engaging game experiences—from worldbuilding and character design to interactive UI/UX flows. When I’m not sketching medieval landscapes or refining animation workflows, you’ll find me experimenting with new prompt-engineering techniques or diving into planetary science readings.
