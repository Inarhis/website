No Car, No Problem

A fast-paced momentum game inspired by UCSC’s real transportation struggles and classic mobile titles like Tiny Wings. Players control a pixel-art bus racing through hilly roads, balancing speed and timing to reach each stop efficiently while maintaining momentum and fuel. Built in Unity with handcrafted pixel environments and original sound design, it turns everyday commuting into an exciting challenge.

Concept art of the player’s bus and initial map sketches.
Testing slopes and jump physics during early playtests.
Early UI layout and speed meter design.
Unity prototype build for bus acceleration and momentum.
Final gameplay build showcasing environment polish and score tracking.
