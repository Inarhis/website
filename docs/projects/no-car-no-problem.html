No Car, No Problem!

No Car, No Problem! is a cozy pixel-art project built collaboratively by our team. I created the main in-game art and title assets and handled the primary environment pieces (including the bus and bus-stop/bench). Throughout production I attended every meeting, kept communication flowing on Discord, and made iterative updates from playtest feedback—such as correcting scale, reworking the bus dimensions to match the engine, adjusting headlamps, and tuning color saturation to the creative direction. We chose a pixel style for efficiency and clarity, and I executed my assets primarily in Procreate (other artists used Aseprite). Influences included Stardew Valley, Kindergarten, and The Escapists, which guided our palette, shading limits, and cozy tone.
