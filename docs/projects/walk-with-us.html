Walk With Us

Walk With Us is a narrative-driven exploration project focusing on emotional storytelling and creature interaction. Players journey through haunting environments inhabited by mythical beings, each reflecting fragments of the world’s forgotten past. My work focused on the creature, environment, and background art, each piece designed to capture the atmosphere of mystery, melancholy, and quiet beauty that defines the game’s tone and worldbuilding.

Game Art

A shadowed bear stands at the edge of the woods, embodying the silent tension of nature and balance.

A single glowing gem lies among coal, symbolizing resilience and light within decay.

A fox spirit glows softly in the ruins, guiding travelers through the lingering echoes of memory.

The deserted town stands still beneath a muted sky, a relic of civilization reclaimed by silence.

A windswept graveyard nestled in the canyon, a solemn resting place for those lost to time.
