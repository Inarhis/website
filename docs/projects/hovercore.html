Hovercore

Hovercore is a collaborative 3D world-building and gameplay prototype exploring the design and atmosphere of a futuristic racing environment. Developed as part of a short-term environment and modeling demo, the project emphasizes fast movement, environmental composition, prop detailing, and lighting design. The resulting prototype captures both scale and momentum, establishing a foundation for future hover-based exploration games.
