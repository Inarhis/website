Hovercore

A high-speed arena racing prototype where players pilot customizable hovercrafts through neon-lit circuits. Designed to emphasize tight controls, aerial drift mechanics, and reactive lighting, Hovercore captures the adrenaline of futuristic racing with fluid motion, precise handling, and vibrant visuals powered by Unity.
