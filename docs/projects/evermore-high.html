The Mystery of Evermore High

The Mystery of Evermore High is an interactive narrative game where players uncover hidden secrets and systemic corruption lurking beneath the surface of a seemingly ordinary high school. Designed in Twine, the game uses branching dialogue pathways, tension-building narrative devices, and player-choice consequences to immerse the user in an unfolding psychological mystery. I created the core narrative architecture, pacing structure, and UI layout to enhance narrative clarity and emotional impact. The minimalist visual presentation places full focus on decision-making, allowing players to shape their own version of the truth while navigating themes of trust, identity, and institutional power.
